HoundDog

NRyo Inui (犬いぬ井い猟りょう Inui Ryō?),also known as the Hunting Dog Hero: Hound Dog (猟犬ヒーローハウンドドッグ Ryōken Hīrō Haundodoggu?), is a Pro Hero, and the Lifestyle Guidance Counselor at U.A. High School.

Quirk

Skilled Youth Counselor: As a high school guidance counselor, it is implied that he is skilled in helping youths deal with their problems. Quirk Dog (犬いぬ Inu?): Ryo's Quirk gives him the attributes and abilities of a dog, such as a heightened sense of smell.
