LunchRush

Cook Hero: Lunch Rush (クックヒーロー ランチラッシュ Kukku Hīrō Ranchi Rasshu?)[1] is a Pro Hero and a Chef at the cafeteria of U.A. High School.

Quirk

Cooking: Judging by the way the students eat their food, we can assume that Lunch Rush is a very good cook, and Ochaco says she never gets tired of the food at U.A. because of the sheer variety of food he offers. Six years ago, he prepared a full-course French meal for ten-thousand victims of a typhoon on his lonesome, showing that he can prepare a vast amount of food in a short period of time. His culinary skills should not be underestimated. Quirk Unknown Quirk: Lunch Rush possesses a Quirk of unknown name and unknown nature.
